About NutriMed

NutriMed is a state-of-the-art clinical nutrition management system designed for dietitians and healthcare providers. It provides automated calculations for metabolic baseline (BMR) and total energy expenditure (TDEE) using peer-reviewed physiological equations.

Compliance & Algorithms

  • Mifflin-St Jeor Equation: Current gold standard for non-obese and obese adults.
  • Harris-Benedict: Classic clinical metabolic estimation.
  • WHO/FAO Standards: Activity factor multipliers based on official healthcare guidelines.
